> The BioHackathon in Japan, hosted by the Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS), prioritizes research topics that focus
> on the integrated use of databases in life sciences. Our focus is continuously on the standardization and interoperability
> of life sciences and biomedical databases (knowledge graphs). Simultaneously, by combining innovative approaches with
> information from literature, we aim to enable new discoveries through the development of methods and applications in data
> science.
